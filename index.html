Research学术研究

How Does Corporate Social Responsibility Affect Brand Equity? 企业社会责任如何影响品牌资产？——来自二手车市场的证据

Job Market Paper

Draft available upon request 论文草稿可应询提供

This paper employs a Difference-in-Differences (DiD) strategy to analyze market response to VW's court-mandated Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) investment plan. 本文采用双重差分法 (DiD)，利用大众汽车 (VW) 法院强制执行的零排放汽车 (ZEV) 投资计划作为外生冲击，分析市场的动态反应。

Key findings: Finds a significant 13.5% increase in final auction prices of VW used cars, suggesting non-voluntary CSR initiatives can enhance consumer willingness to pay for brand equity. 主要发现： 大众二手车的最终拍卖价格显著上涨了 13.5%。这表明非自愿（法规驱动）的企业社会责任举措依然能够提升消费者对品牌资产的支付意愿。

Information Acquisition in Online Marketplaces: Evidence from eBay 在线市场的搜寻与信息获取：来自eBay的证据

Working Paper

Investigates the impact of buyer information acquisition through eBay's WeGoLook inspection service. Empirical results reveal that the service increased final auction prices by approx. 12% and reduced the marginal value of seller-provided photos. Demonstrates a strategic supply-side response where sellers increased voluntary disclosure. 研究 eBay "WeGoLook" 检验服务对买家信息获取的影响。实证结果显示，该服务使最终拍卖价格提高了约 12%，并降低了卖家提供照片的边际价值。研究还发现了供给侧的策略性反应，即卖家会增加自愿披露以应对第三方验证的威胁。

The Impacts of Central Bank Digital Currency on Banking Industry 央行数字货币对银行业的影响

Work in Progress

Develops a theoretical framework integrating the OLG model with monopolistic banking to investigate the trade-offs between CBDC privacy protection, financial inclusion, and bank credit supply. 构建了一个结合 OLG 模型与垄断银行的理论框架，探讨央行数字货币 (CBDC) 在隐私保护、金融普惠和银行信贷供给之间的权衡。
