Research

How Does Corporate Social Responsibility Affect Brand Equity?

Job Market Paper

Draft available upon request

This paper employs a Difference-in-Differences (DiD) strategy to analyze market response to VW's court-mandated Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) investment plan.

Key findings: Finds a significant 13.5% increase in final auction prices of VW used cars, suggesting non-voluntary CSR initiatives can enhance consumer willingness to pay for brand equity.

Working Papers & Work in Progress

Information Acquisition in Online Marketplaces: Evidence from eBay

Working Paper

Investigates the impact of buyer information acquisition through eBay's WeGoLook inspection service. Empirical results reveal that the service increased final auction prices by approx. 12% and reduced the marginal value of seller-provided photos. Demonstrates a strategic supply-side response where sellers increased voluntary disclosure.

The Impacts of Central Bank Digital Currency on Banking Industry

Work in Progress

Develops a theoretical framework integrating the OLG model with monopolistic banking to investigate the trade-offs between CBDC privacy protection, financial inclusion, and bank credit supply.
